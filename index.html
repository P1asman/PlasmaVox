About

The Voice Behind The Worlds

For years I’ve entertained friends and fellow gamers with immersive character voices and cinematic storytelling around the table. Now it’s time to take that passion professional.

PlasmaVox offers high-end cinematic voice acting for games, trailers and immersive media. My fully treated home studio features the industry-standard Shure SM7B microphone — paired with clean gain staging and professional signal chain — delivering broadcast-quality recordings at 48kHz / 24-bit.

I specialise in fantasy atmospheres, emotionally intense characters, powerful narration and memorable performances that elevate your project. From menacing villains to hopeful heroes, every delivery is crafted with clarity, depth and that signature cinematic edge.
